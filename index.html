Hico is Pakistan’s first ice cream company. Established in 1952, we now offer many more flavors in many more locations across the country. Ever since, we’ve been devoted to making the best ice cream in Pakistan for you!We manufacture 100% pure dairy ice cream. Our ice cream is made from dairy ingredients such as fresh cream and milk, is free of vegetable oil, and contains no saturated fats. We only procure the freshest and purest ingredients to make the best quality ice cream.
